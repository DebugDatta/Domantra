# Domantra

Domantra is a career domain assessment that helps you understand where you naturally fit across different types of work.

Try it here  
https://domantra.netlify.app

## What you get

• A short guided assessment  
• A ranked breakdown across ten career domains  
• A clear visual results dashboard  
• Detailed role pages with real world context  

Each role includes day to day work, required skills, degree alignment, and fit indicators.

## How it works

• You answer twenty multiple choice questions  
• Each answer contributes to multiple career domains  
• Your total scores are calculated and normalized  
• You receive a ranked view of your strongest domains  

The system is structured and score driven. It is not random and does not rely on vague personality labels.

## Career domains covered

• Consulting and Strategy  
• Sales and Business Development  
• Marketing and Branding  
• Content and Creative  
• Finance and Investment  
• Product and Growth  
• Operations and Process  
• Founders Office  
• Data and Analytics  
• Human Resources  

## What makes it different

• No generic advice  
• No personality type labels  
• No sign up or login  
• No data collection  

Everything runs directly in your browser.

## Privacy

• Your data never leaves your device  
• Progress is saved locally so you can continue anytime  

## How to use

• Open the app in your browser  
• Start the assessment  
• Answer all questions honestly  
• Review your results  
• Explore each domain in detail

## How to approach the assessment

• Answer based on what you would actually choose  
• Do not overthink individual questions  
• Focus on your top few domains rather than just one  

## What this is

• A structured way to explore career fit  
• A decision support tool  
• A starting point for deeper exploration  

## What this is not

• A final answer to your career  
• A personality test  
• A substitute for real world experience  

## Final note

Domantra is built to give you clarity

Use it to understand your direction and then validate it through real work conversations and experimentation
